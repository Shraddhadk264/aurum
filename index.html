About Theia Cafe

Our Story

It all started in a small kitchen, where homestyle recipes were cooked with love and shared with family. Soon, neighbors gathered too, drawn by the warmth of food that felt like home.
In 2022, this passion grew into a dream—a café where every plate carries the same comfort, and every guest feels like family.
